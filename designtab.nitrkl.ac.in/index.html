"Design Tab"-the designing club of NIT Rourkela.
It not just a name but an identity of all the design enthusiasts and creative minds who think of something new,something out of the box. Born on 2013, in National Institute of Technology,Rourkela, Design Tab has evolved not only as a club but a mentor for all those young, upcoming designers.

Design Tab aims to bring together all aspects of designing under a single hood and create a productive environment for young designers. Every moment new products, new designs emerge and old ones fade away. We take this challenge of keeping pace with the changing trends of the society and keeping the spirit of “good, fresh, creative design” alive.
